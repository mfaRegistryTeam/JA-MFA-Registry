Barbadian MFA National Registration System

Welcome to the official Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade of Barbados National Registration Service. This site is geared towards Barbadians: Travelling abroad, resident overseas and friends of Barbados. In light of recent global events it has become evident that it is necessary to strengthen our connections with citizens. We therefore encourage anyone who may fall into any of the abovementioned categories to provide us with information to improve both the response in times of crises and the quality of service.
