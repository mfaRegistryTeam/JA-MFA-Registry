REGISTRATION OF JAMAICAN CITIZENS ON SHORT TERM VISITS ABROAD

Message from the Minister


Fellow Jamaicans, Welcome to the new online portal for Jamaicans travelling. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade has developed this portal with an important main goal.
First, as we all learned from the COVID-19 pandemic, smooth communication between the government and nationals overseas in difficult times is vital. Registering on this portal will allow the Ministry to get in touch with you when you are abroad, especially if any crises emerge.


Senator Hon. Kamina Johnson Smith
Minister of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade
