Barbadian MFA National Registration System

Welcome to the official Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade of Barbados National Registration Service. This site is geared towards Barbadians: Travelling abroad, resident overseas and friends of Barbados. In light of recent global events it has become evident that it is necessary to strengthen our connections with citizens. We therefore encourage anyone who may fall into any of the abovementioned categories to provide us with information to improve both the response in times of crises and the quality of service.

Message from the Minister


Fellow Barbadians, Welcome to the new online portal for Barbadians overseas. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade has developed this portal with two main goals.
First, as we all learned from the COVID19 pandemic, smooth communication between the government and nationals overseas in difficult times is vital. Registering on this portal will allow the Ministry to get in touch with you when you are abroad and if any crises emerge.
Second, this portal is a continuation of the numerous efforts we have made over the years to foster the closest possible relationship between Barbados and our diaspora. If you live overseas, this portal will allow you to find opportunities that will bring us all even closer together.


Jerome X. Walcott
Minister of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade
